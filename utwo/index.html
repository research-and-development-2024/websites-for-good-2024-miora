About Our Projects

MiOra provides opportunities for students passionate about health and STEM to drive change in public health, from cancer awareness to vaccine advocacy, led by renowned experts.

Disparities in Cancer Programs

This program trains CSULA public health students to enhance cancer awareness and trial access in underserved areas where we are committed to healthcare equity and cancer outreach.


Immigrant, Health Literacy & Access Project

Partnering with Oakland University, MiOra's project focuses on improving healthcare understanding and access for immigrants. By assessing attitudes and behaviors and identifying barriers to access, we aim to promote health equity and inclusivity within immigrant communities.

Assessing US Mens Health Resource Utilization & the Associated Barriers Project

MiOra engages Health Ambassadors to understand men's healthcare knowledge and barriers across the US. Through data collection and publication, we aim to improve healthcare accessibility for men nationwide.


AI Project

Exploring AI's impact on healthcare, MiOra aims to enhance patient care and resource allocation. By keeping up with AI advancements, we contribute to developing effective healthcare solutions.

Mental Health Project

Focusing on peer-to-peer programs for stress management among students, MiOra enhances mental health support in educational settings.


Healthy Long-Life Project: Preventative Care

Promoting preventive care through education on vaccines, screening tests, and healthy lifestyles, MiOra empowers individuals to take charge of their health.

Policy Project

MiOra trains Health Ambassadors in healthcare policy and advocacy through expert partnerships and learning sessions. Join us in the discussion on critical health issues.

OUR IMPACT

Community Health Improvement

"Closing the Disparities in Cancer Program" and "Mental Health Project" directly improve community health outcomes. MiOra's initiatives address critical health issues, providing resources, awareness, and support to underserved populations.

Innovative Solutions

MiOra's projects involve cutting-edge research, leading to creative solutions and advancements in healthcare. Whether exploring AI applications or conducting cancer research, MiOra pushes the boundaries of what is possible in healthcare.

Policy Impact

MiOra's "Policy Project" shapes health policies. By engaging in policy initiatives, students and researchers influence the development of frameworks that can have a lasting impact on public health practices and regulations.

Empowering Vulnerable Populations

Initiatives like the "Immigrant Health Literacy & Access Project" focus on empowering vulnerable populations with the knowledge and resources needed to access healthcare services, thereby improving individual health outcomes and addressing systemic issues related to healthcare disparities.
